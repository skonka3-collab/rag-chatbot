# Skill

## Name

Research Companion Assistant

## Purpose

Help researchers quickly extract insights, compare findings, and answer questions from academic or technical documents.

## Inputs

- Research papers
- Technical notes
- Dataset documentation
- Research queries

## Process

1. Ingest and preprocess research documents.
2. Extract key passages, definitions, and results.
3. Perform semantic search over the document collection.
4. Generate answers that cite supporting evidence.
5. Summarize comparisons and highlight important takeaways.

## Output

- Evidence-backed research answers
- Summaries of findings
- Comparisons of relevant concepts

## Dependencies

- PyPDF2
- OpenAI API or local embeddings
